Baked Tomato Slices

Home

Description

Baked Tomato Slices are a simple yet delicious side dish that highlights the natural sweetness of tomatoes, enhanced with olive oil, herbs, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the tomatoes into thick slices.
  3. Place the tomato slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top of each slice.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Serve warm as a side dish or appetizer.
